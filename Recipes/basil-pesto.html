Basil Pesto

Favourite pesto recipe courtesy of BBC Good Food

Ingredients:

  • 50g pine nuts or cashews
  • large bunch of basil, leaves picked
  • 50g parmesan or vegetarian alternative
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 100ml olive oil

Steps:

  1. Toast the nuts in a pan over a low heat. Tip into a mini chopper with the remaining ingredients, or use a large pestle and mortar, then blitz or pound into a rough sauce. Will keep for three days in the fridge.
